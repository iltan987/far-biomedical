# About Us

**FAR Better Bio** is an R&amp;D company developing advanced blood-cell separation and apheretic blood filtration technologies. Our work focuses on two core platforms:

- **AutoCeive™** – an automated benchtop blood-cell separation device designed for rapid, sterile, and efficient cell enrichment from fluid laboratory samples.
- **AphereCeive™** – a programmable, cell-specific apheretic collection system enabling targeted isolation of immune and circulating cells directly from continuously processed blood.

Our R&amp;D operations are based in **Middle East Technical University Technopark (METU Teknokent)** and **METU Research Center (BIOMATEN)**, where we collaborate with multidisciplinary engineers and scientists.

In addition to our core research, **we actively distribute laboratory devices, consumables and materials** for **supporting our R&amp;D pipeline**, by ensuring access to cutting-edge instruments and high-quality materials.
